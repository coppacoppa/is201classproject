Types of Climbing

There are many types of climbing, as shown in the graph above

  1. Ice Climbing
    • Done on frozen waterfalls or ice formations
    • Uses ice axes and crampons
    • Requires cold-weather gear
  2. Traditional (Trad) Climbing
    • Done on natural rock formations
    • Climbers place their own protection (cams, nuts, etc.)
    • Focuses on route finding skills
  3. Sport Climbing
    • Pre-placed bolts for protection
    • Focus on strength and technique
    • Common at outdoor crags
  4. Bouldering
    • Short climbs without rope
    • Uses crash pads for safety
    • Focus on power and problem-solving
  5. Indoor Climbing
    • Done in climbing gyms
    • Controlled environment
    • Great for beginners
  6. Free Soloing
    • No ropes or protection
    • Extremely dangerous
    • Requires elite skill and mental control
